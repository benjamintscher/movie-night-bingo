Villa House Rules

  1. Pick your card before the “previously on.” No swapping once the recap starts — loyalty matters here, unlike in the villa.
  2. First bingo (row, column, or diagonal) wins the night. You must yell “I got a text!” before your win counts. Mumbling voids it.
  3. Disputed squares: pause the episode, everyone with a stake pleads their case in 15 seconds or less, then a group vote — the person whose square it is doesn’t get to vote. Majority rules, live with it.
  4. Group square: whenever Sincere sheds a tear, all four of you take a sip whether it’s on your card or not. House tradition. Non-negotiable.
  5. Blackout bonus: first full card wins the night outright and picks the next watch-party snacks. Everyone else fights over second.
  6. No bingo by the final credits? Most squares marked wins; ties go to a group vote (yes, campaigning is allowed).
